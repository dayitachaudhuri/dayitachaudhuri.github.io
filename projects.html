Decoding Keyboard Strokes with Sound and Language

CoAtNet • Large Language Models • LoRA • Python

Used CoAtNet for keystroke classification, achieving 93.9% accuracy on individual keystrokes. Naive concatenation gave only 15% sentence-level accuracy. Introducing a second-stage LLM—either a LoRA-tuned or larger Llama model—improved sentence-level inference by over 5x. Showed that compact, targeted fine-tuning can rival larger models in contextual error correction.

Intra Cranial Haemorrhage (ICH) Detection from Brain CT Images

CNN (SE-ResNeXT) • Bidirectional LSTM • GradCAM • Python

Trained a model combining SE-ResNeXT and bidirectional LSTM to classify brain CT images into 5 ICH subtypes. Integrated advanced preprocessing, feature extraction, and explainable AI via Grad-CAM. Achieved >90% accuracy for well-represented classes and ~50% for underrepresented classes, comparable to SOTA performance.
